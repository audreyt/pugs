The Perl 6 Language Cross Operators Forms every possible permutation of two or more lists This is a special case; can stick an operator in-between two Xs (1,2) X (3,4) # ((1,3),(1,4),(2,3),(2,4)) # If @user_facts contains words relating to # a user, can concatenate all possible # combinations of them together – test for # weak passwords. :-) my @guesses = @user_facts X~X @user_facts;
